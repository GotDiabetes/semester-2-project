Second Semester Project

A cutting-edge smart mirror project that utilizes a computer monitor combined with a wooden frame and a one-way mirror. This innovative design allows users to view digital information on the screen while also being able to see their reflection. To enhance image quality while keeping costs low, I plan to use plastic acrylic instead of traditional glass, ensuring a clearer and more affordable display.
