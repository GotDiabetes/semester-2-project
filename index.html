Project Timeline

Week 1-2 (Jan 24 - Feb 7): Software Setup & Research

Setting Up the MagicMirror² Framework

Our first step is installing Raspberry Pi OS and configuring the MagicMirror² framework, an open-source platform for building smart mirrors. This ensures a functional foundation before adding advanced features. We will also test different modules to confirm their compatibility with our planned customizations.

Researching Smart Mirror Enhancements

We are exploring advanced features such as voice activation, touch interactivity, real-time weather updates, and external API integrations. Learning JavaScript is a key focus, as it is necessary for customizing MagicMirror² modules. Additionally, we are reviewing different smart mirror projects to identify successful implementations and potential improvements.

Understanding the Benefits of Smart Mirrors

Studies, such as this one (link), highlight the advantages of smart mirrors, including their potential in health monitoring, personalized user experiences, and hands-free accessibility. These insights guide our design decisions. We are also considering how smart mirrors can integrate with healthcare applications for enhanced usability.
