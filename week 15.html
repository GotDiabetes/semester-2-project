Smart Mirror Project Demo

This project combines the reflection of a two-way glass mirror with an interactive display, allowing users to view videos, check the weather, time, calendar events, and more—all while seeing themselves in the mirror. Perfect for beauty tutorials or daily info at a glance.

Demo Video

Main Features

  • Display any YouTube video directly on the smart mirror.
  • Live weather updates with location-based data.
  • Digital clock with real-time seconds.
  • Date and period end-time countdown display.
  • Custom calendar with symbols, specific dates, and times.
  • Two-way glass mirror allows reflection + screen visibility simultaneously.
  • Ideal for makeup tutorials, allowing users to see themselves and follow along.
