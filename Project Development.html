Project Development - Early Stages

Connecting the Raspberry Pi

We have successfully connected our Raspberry Pi to a monitor, which serves as the foundation for our smart mirror project. This setup allows us to display various pieces of information dynamically.

Installing Essential Modules

We have integrated various GitHub modules and applications that enable our system to display critical data. These include:

  • A digital calendar that syncs with our online schedules, ensuring we never miss important events.
  • Real-time weather updates, showing temperature, humidity, and weather conditions based on our location.
  • Custom widgets that can be expanded in future developments for added functionality.

Challenges with Wireless Connectivity

One of the primary challenges we encountered is maintaining a stable wireless connection. Our system relies heavily on internet access, and any drop in connectivity disrupts data retrieval and display.

Impact of Network Instability

When the Wi-Fi signal weakens or disconnects, several issues arise:

  • The calendar fails to update, leading to outdated event displays.
  • Weather information may become inaccurate or fail to load entirely.
  • Daily news headlines, which are retrieved from external sources, become scrambled or disappear.

To address this issue, we are exploring options such as using a more robust Wi-Fi adapter, implementing a network reconnection script, or considering an Ethernet-based solution for stability.
